

需要考虑查询出来数据量的多少
query template:
1. 1 word query
fts_match_word("Abraham", a)
2. 2 Word Union Query
fts_match_word("Abraham", a) or fts_match_word("Lincoln", a)
3. 2 Word Intersection Query
fts_match_word("Abraham", a) and fts_match_word("Lincoln", a)
4. 2field-2word-union-query
fts_match_word("Abraham", a) or fts_match_word("Lincoln", b)
5. 2field-2word-intersection-query
fts_match_word("Abraham", a) and fts_match_word("Lincoln", b)
